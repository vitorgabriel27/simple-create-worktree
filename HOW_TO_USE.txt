HOW TO USE
==========

Canonical docs live under `docs/`.

Recommended commands:
- git-create-worktree
- git-update-worktree
- git-merge-worktree
- git-remove-worktree
- git-worktree-sync

Examples:
- git-create-worktree feat/login-page
- git-create-worktree -b feat/new-dashboard develop
- git-update-worktree feat/login-page
- git-update-worktree feat/login-page --to refs/tags/v1.2.3
- git-merge-worktree refs/remotes/origin/develop feat/login-page
- git-remove-worktree feat/login-page
- git-worktree-sync /path/to/worktree
